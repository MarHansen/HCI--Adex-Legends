When Wraith was 6 years old, she was sucked in the dark dimesion portal. Wraith was greeted by the dark dimension lord, sakar. Wraith having a difficult time trying to get back to her own dimension. She struggled for 60 years trying to find her way out untill she discovered the dark dimesion power called “Dimension Drift” and used it to open the portal.
